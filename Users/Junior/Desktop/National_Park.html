The Majestic American Bison

Found in Yellowstone National Park

The American Bison is a very large herbivore, Kind of like a big fluffy cow. It has 2 horns on the side of its head and has long shaggy brown fur. They are commonly referred to as buffalo. They can be found all throughout Yellowstone but specifically in the plains where there is lots of yummy grass to eat. Bison are the largest mammal in North America.
